Photography Manipulation

The following two projects have different vibes but share my patience and appreciation for editing photography seamlessly. I am familiar with shooting film and digital photography as well as studio setup and lifestyle photography.




These optical illusion portraits were created by masking multiple photographs together to create a unique view.

This was a project created while I was in my undergrad program that required me to 'change the past'. When I was younger, my mom would always take multipe photographs of the same event to ensure she had a great shot. I chose to scan in these old photographs and mask these images so it appears I had a twin.
