ABOUT ME

Amanda is a digital designer based in Brooklyn, NY. Originally from Connecticut, she studied graphic design at Southern Connecticut State University, photography & advertising while abroad at Napier University in Edinburgh, UK, and Front-End Web Development at General Assembly (which allowed her the skills to build this site!). Her interests include iced coffee year-round, fashion, being an ebay enthusiast, and discovering the best food around New York City. She has attention to detail, strong work ethic, and a sense of humor.
