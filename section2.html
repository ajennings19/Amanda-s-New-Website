PetPlate Preferred
2:1 Campaign

Info

Description
With PetPlate’s switch in manufacturers and cooking techniques, this marketing campaign was developed to showcase the improvements made to the meals with more noticeable chunks of meat and vegetables. To support this narrative, a focus group consisting of dogs of different ages, sizes, and breeds was conducted, and the results showed the majority of dogs preferred the new recipe by a ratio of 2:1.

To visually convey these enhancements, the meals top-down are shown prominently, making it the main focus and providing a close-up view of the improved formula.
