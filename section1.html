Description
PetPlate offers a selection of human-grade, vet-designed, filler-free, fresh wholesome ingredient food options for dogs. One of their signature products, supplements, was in need of a rebrand to accurately represent their beneficial and functional ingredients apart from dog treats.

As the lead designer, I executed the supplements packaging rebrand while introducing two new SKUs and developed a unique look / feel to this product line, diversifying its offerings.

The goal of this packaging re-brand was to shift from a previous packaging style and naming that resembled dog treats to a new design and positioning that accurately conveyed the products as functional soft chew supplements with beneficial ingredients.
