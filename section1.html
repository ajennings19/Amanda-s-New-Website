Description
PetPlate provides a range of human-grade, vet-designed, filler-free, fresh, and wholesome food options for dogs. To distinguish their supplements from dog treats and showcase their beneficial and functional ingredients, the company decided to rebrand this signature product.

As the lead designer, I rebranded the supplements packaging, introducing two new SKUs, and creating a unique look and feel for the product line, diversifying and expanding PetPlate's core offerings.

The goal of this packaging rebrand was to move away from the previous dog treat-like style and naming, and instead, adopt a new design and positioning that clearly communicated the products as functional soft chew supplements with beneficial ingredients.
